Hotel Wellington

Located in the well-known neighborhood Salamanca, only 20 minutes by walking to La Puerta del Sol and in front of Parque del Retiro. This luxury hotel has a wide variety of shops, restaurants and bars.

The elegant and stylish rooms offer accommodation with free WiFi, flat-screen TV and private bathroom.

With magnificent views, The Wellington Club offers to our club customers, personalized courtesy service of breakfast, soft drinks and snacks. Its greatest charm, are the outdoor pool and jacuzzi on season. They offer a wellness center Le Max Wellness Wellington Club with SPA, fitness room and Health and Beauty services.
